About Flashworld

Flashworld is dedicated to creating a global trade ecosystem that connects businesses, customers, and governments for sustainable development.

Vision

To lead global trade by 2035, driving sustainable economic growth and fostering seamless international collaboration.

Mission

To create an inclusive economic framework, enabling nations and businesses to thrive within our ecosystem, while reducing poverty and creating millions of jobs worldwide.

Our Ecosystem

The Flashworld ecosystem is designed to support and nurture businesses at every stage of their growth:

  • Business Incubation
  • Digital Infrastructure
  • Logistics Support
  • Financial Assistance
  • Global Market Access

Our Impact

Flashworld is making a significant impact on global trade and local economies:

  • 15 million group businesses
  • 150 million personal businesses in Zambia alone
  • Job creation on a massive scale
  • Poverty reduction through sustainable business models
  • Improved national economies and international trade

Our Partners

Flashworld collaborates with a wide range of partners to achieve its goals:

  • National Governments
  • International Organizations
  • Global Charities
  • Financial Institutions
  • Technology Companies
